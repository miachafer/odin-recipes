Cuba Libre

Description

Cuba Libre is one of the most famous cocktails from Cuba and translates to "Free Cuba". It's said that the it was created during the Spanish-American War by a U.S. Army captain in Havana that mixed his Bacardí rum with Coca-Cola and lime.

Specialists say that Cuba Libre is actually different from Rum & Coke cocktail because the classic rum for Cuba Libre is Bacardí. One of the reasons for Cuba Libre's international success is that its ingredients can be found anywhere in the world.

Ingredients

  • 30 ml of rum
  • 90 ml of Coca-Cola
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice, then add rum and Coca-Cola.
  2. Squeeze the lime into your drink (optional).
  3. Garnish with a lime wedge.
  4. ¡Por Cuba Libre!
