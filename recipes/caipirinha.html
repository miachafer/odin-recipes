Caipirinha

Description

Caipirinha is a popular Brazilian cocktail. It's said that was invented as a medicine for Spanish Flu. The medicine drink was originally made with honey and garlic (yikes).

Nowadays, the recipe of Caipirinha does not include garlic or honey. The alcoholic ingredient of Caipirinha is cachaça, a Brazilian spirit similar to tequila, but it is made from sugarcane.

Ingredients

  • Cachaça
  • Tahiti Lime
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cut the lime in wedges.
  2. In a double rocks glass, muddle the lime wedges and sugar.
  3. Fill the glass with ice, add the cachaça, and stir briefly.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel (optional)
  5. You can use a straw to delight yourself with your caipirinha.
  6. Have fun!
